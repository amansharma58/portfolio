About Me

I am Aman Sharma, currently working as a Lab Technician in the Humanities and Social Science Department at Jaypee Institute of Information Technology. In this role, I manage and maintain lab systems, ensure smooth network accessibility for students, and assist learners in resolving technical issues so they can focus on their studies without interruptions.

Previously, I worked as a Front-End Developer at Smanik Technologies, where I specialized in building interactive and dynamic websites using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, and WordPress. I also contributed to developing innovative features, such as dynamic sections and mobile-friendly designs, which improved overall user engagement.

With a blend of technical expertise and hands-on academic support experience, my goal is to contribute to both technological innovation and educational growth, bridging the gap between development and learning environments.
